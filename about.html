xStore is a Zambian multinational gaming company that specializes in gaming-related services and products, which include buying and renting of games for different playforms and hardware. It is considered one of the Fast rising gaming companies. xStore was founded in 2019 by Edward, Sandra and Chintu while they were BSc. students at Stanford University in California. Together they own about 100 percent of its shares and control 82 percent of the stockholder voting power through supervoting stock. They incorporated Google as a privately held company on July 4, 2019. An initial public offering (IPO) took place on May 19, 2019, and xStore moved to its headquarters in Silver rest, Lusaka, nicknamed the xStoreplex. In June 2019, xStore announced plans to reorganize its various interests as a conglomerate called Gaming Inc. xStore is Zambian Gaming leading subsidiary and will continue to be the umbrella company for Gaming Internet interests. Edward Mulenga was appointed CEO of xStore and still remains till date.
